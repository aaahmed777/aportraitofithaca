##  This collections project aims to digitally archive and exhibit the graffiti, political posters, and street art of Ithaca. I believe that one can learn a great deal about a city through stickers plastered onto its poles, art and political slogans painted over its walls, and remnants of community organizing taped onto various surfaces all over the city. These practices are typically ephemeral in nature: they come and they go, disappearing just as quick as they arrive. Through using digital technologies varying from film and photography to scanning technologies this project aims to capture, preserve, and exhibit that which is transient - soon to be painted over or even erased by agents of the state. 


If you are interested in further exploring visual art in Ithaca check out and support local resources @Ithacamurals and @ithagraff on Instagram!
